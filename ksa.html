KSA Analysis

1. Examine the JD

Scrutinize the Job Description to identify Knowledge, Skills, Attributes (KSAs)

2. Classify Your Skills

Technical Skills Transferable Skills
Skills that are necessary to perform a particular job, often can be proven with certifications or degree Skills that can be broadly applied to other jobs
  • Coding
  • Laboratory Techniques
  • Research Pedagogies
  • Communication
  • Time Management
  • Leadership
  • Problem-solving
  • Multi-tasking

3. Match the KSA

1. List down 3-5 skills required for the role you are interested in
2. Identify 1-2 experience(s) which can be matched to a specific skill or attribute required in the JD
