STAR Framework

What is STAR?

Situation
在什么背景下
Task
要达到什么目标
Action
我的工作是什么
Result
结果/收获是什么

How to Use STAR

1. Use strong action verbs

2. Elaborate on what you did

Write a short description of what you did
Include tools or software used (C++, Matlab, Access..)

… developed marketing plan …
… created a database using Ms Access …
… proposed a business plan …
… liaised with external vendors …

3. Strengthen with results

Talk about your achievement
Quantify where possible
Show the Rationale behind your actions

… to enhance visibility of the products on display.
… to provide analysis for decision making.
… to ensure reduction of equipment downtime.

STAR Examples

  • Led a team of 20 student volunteers to teach English to 60 orphans in Siem Reap, Cambodia.
  • Initiated and launched a campus-wide project for Food Bank, collected and distributed 200kg of non-perishable goods to needy families.
  • Project-managed a team of 4 members to launch a fund-raising campaign which raised $1000 for the victims of the Taiwan earthquake.
  • Designed marketing materials to recruit volunteers for the Freshmen Orientation Camp and recruited 30 more new members.
  • Conceptualised the booth design for the Volunteer Fest, successfully attracted 500 sign-ups in one day.
  • Researched and presented a case study about the effects of Global Warming on China's marine biodiversity, receiving an A+ for the module.
